Services · 01

Disaster Recovery

Replication strategies across secondary data centers and cloud — designed, governed and operated.

The challenge

When the primary site fails, critical services must come back — within an agreed time, with agreed data loss, without improvisation.

Our approach

Policy-driven replication strategy: architecture design, secondary-site resource planning, job configuration, evaluation, training and documentation.

Technology

Azure Site Recovery, VMware Site Recovery Manager, Zerto, Veeam Replication, storage replication, Oracle Data Guard, SQL Server Always On.

Business outcome

Recovery objectives met and provable — the business keeps running as if nothing happened.

Design & Implementation

Architecture design, resource planning, replication job configuration, evaluation, training and documentation.

Policy & Assessment

Defining RPO and RTO, building recovery scenarios and runbooks, developing OLAs, and assessing existing environments.

Managed Replication

Operating and monitoring replication, audit-evidence response, periodic evaluation, reporting and vendor escalation.

Services · 02

Backup & Recovery

Every solution designed around the 3-2-1-1-0 backup rule — verified, restorable, and documented.

The challenge

Backups that are never verified are assumptions. The failure is discovered at the worst possible moment — during a restore.

Our approach

System design, repository sizing, job configuration with verification, recovery validation, training, documentation and tier-2 support.

Technology

Veeam, Veritas NetBackup, Commvault, Rubrik, Cohesity, Dell PowerProtect, IBM Spectrum Protect — matched to fit, not familiarity.

Business outcome

Verified, restorable copies of what the business cannot afford to lose — with evidence for auditors.

Backup & Recovery Build

System design, repository sizing, job configuration with verification, recovery validation, training, docs and tier-2 support.

Policy & Assessment

Building or improving backup policies: RPO/RTO per service, recovery scenarios, operator-level OLAs, and environment assessment.

Managed Backup

Continuous operation and monitoring, audit-evidence response, periodic evaluation, status reporting and tier-3 vendor escalation.
