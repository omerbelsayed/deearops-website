Solutions

Start from what your business cannot afford to lose.

Every DeeAROps solution begins with a business question, not a product. Find your situation below — then see exactly how we take it from risk to guarantee.

Situation 01

"Our critical services aren't properly protected yet."

Business challenge

Critical IT services are running without a verified backup or replication system — every day is an unquantified risk.

DeeAROps approach

The Build engagement: study your services, size and propose the solution, build and verify the jobs, train your team, document everything.

Technology

Best-of-breed backup and replication platforms, matched to your infrastructure — designed to the 3-2-1-1-0 rule.

Outcome

A protection system that is sized, configured, verified and documented — with tier-2 support behind it.

Situation 02

"We have backups — but can we actually recover?"

Business challenge

Recovery objectives are undefined or undocumented. Auditors ask for evidence; the honest answer is an assumption.

DeeAROps approach

Policy & Assessment: specify RPO and RTO per service, build recovery scenarios and per-service runbooks, define operator OLAs — or assess and improve the existing environment.

Technology

Grounded in your Business Impact Analysis and internationally recognized frameworks — ISO 22301, NIST CSF, ITIL.

Outcome

Recovery promises that are documented, auditable and testable — for the board, customers and regulators.

See the Policy & Assess scope →

Situation 03

"We can't staff 24/7 operations for backup and DR."

Business challenge

Backup and replication estates need daily operation, monitoring and audit evidence — a specialist function most IT teams cannot staff around the clock.

DeeAROps approach

Managed Service: continuous operation and monitoring, evidence response, periodic evaluation, status reporting and tier-3 vendor escalation. You keep read-only oversight.

Technology

Operated on your platforms with enterprise monitoring toolsets — SolarWinds, PRTG, Zabbix, Grafana, Prometheus, SCOM.

Outcome

A 24/7 DR & backup function measured against contractual SLAs — reported monthly, with service credits behind P1 targets.

See SLA commitments →
