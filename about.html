About DeeAROps

A specialist, not a generalist.

IT failure is not a matter of if — it is a matter of when. DeeAROps exists so that when it happens, the business keeps running as if nothing happened.

Our story

One discipline. Mastered.

DeeAROps is a specialized IT services company focused on one discipline: data protection, backup, disaster recovery and replication.

We help organizations protect critical IT services, ensure business continuity, and meet recovery objectives through policy-driven design, implementation and managed services — across on-premises and cloud environments.

03

Certainty, not hope

Recovery that is tested, documented, monitored — and ready before it is ever needed.
